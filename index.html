Got Questions?

Everything you need to know

What countries does Buildaze serve?
We serve clients globally — with active projects in the USA, UK, UAE, Singapore, Australia, Canada, Germany, the Netherlands and beyond. Our remote-first culture means timezone is never a barrier to great work.
How quickly can you start my project?
Onboarding typically begins within 5–7 business days of signing. We reserve capacity in advance, so the sooner you reach out, the sooner we can lock in your start date.
What's your typical project timeline?
Websites: 4–8 weeks. Shopify / eCommerce builds: 6–12 weeks. SaaS MVPs: 6–8 weeks. Ongoing marketing retainers run month-to-month with 90-day performance reviews.
Do you work with startups or only enterprises?
Both. We work with Series A startups through to global enterprise brands. What matters is ambition and readiness — not company size. Every engagement is scoped to your stage and goals.
What makes Buildaze different from other digital agencies?
Most agencies sell time. We sell outcomes. We're performance-obsessed and equity-minded — we grow alongside your business. Our average client relationship is 5 years because the results keep compounding.
What's the investment for a typical project?
Websites start from $8,000. Shopify stores from $12,000. SaaS MVPs from $24,000. Marketing retainers from $3,500/month. Every project is scoped uniquely — book a free strategy call for a tailored estimate.
